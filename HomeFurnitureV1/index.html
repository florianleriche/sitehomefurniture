Re-sourcing

RE-SOURCE More Suitable Production Capacities

It is crucial that the factories producing your products are a good fit to your business model and take into account your business evolution as well.

This fit need to be re-assessed regularly to give the opportunity to rework on products, supply chain and conditions.


RE-SOURCE Better Prices

Finding suitable production capacity allows to optimize the products and the supply chain, resulting usually in a better price. We have a track record of purchasing gains averaging 27%.

RE-SOURCE Better Quality

Optimizing the product, challenging the raw material sources and be able to work with bill of material allows you to make the necessary adjustments to improve the quality of your products.

RE-SOURCE Better Conditions &Terms

Resourcing for new production capacities allows to assess more carefully for a better collaboration fit and allows to negotiate better terms and conditions.
